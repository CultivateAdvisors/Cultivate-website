About Us

A trusted partner for healthcare innovators

Cultivate Health Advisors is a boutique consultancy founded and led by Amy Andersen, a seasoned healthcare executive with over 25 years of experience spanning innovation, corporate strategy, and growth advisory. The firm works at the intersection of clinical insight, commercial acumen, and industry relationships.

We partner with startups, investors, and established healthcare organizations to drive commercialization, strategic partnerships, and market expansion in digital health, AI, and tech-enabled care delivery. Our work spans the full growth lifecycle — from market entry and product-market fit through scale, fundraising, and strategic partnership development.

What sets us apart is the rare combination of C-suite operational experience across payer, provider, and health tech sectors with an active, high-value network of US healthcare decision-makers, regulators, and investors.

What We Do

Services built for healthcare innovators

We offer a focused suite of advisory services designed to accelerate growth, secure strategic partnerships, and build durable market presence for digital health and health tech companies.

01

Go-to-Market Strategy & Execution

Tailored GTM strategies that align product-market fit with clinical, regulatory, and business value narratives to drive market entry and accelerate adoption.

  • Define product-market fit and priority market segments
  • Craft messaging, white papers, and sales collateral
  • Align GTM with clinical and regulatory narratives
  • Validate entry strategy through stakeholder interviews
02

Strategic Partnerships & Ecosystem Development

Cultivating alliances with health systems, payers, PBMs, digital health companies, and cloud providers to expand distribution and amplify market presence.

  • Identify and cultivate strategic partners
  • Build alliances to expand distribution channels
  • Facilitate pilot programs and co-innovation opportunities
  • Accelerate revenue through partner ecosystems
03

Business Development & Commercial Strategy

Structuring sales organizations and scaling commercial models through fractional BD leadership, executive outreach, and strategic buyer engagement.

  • Sales organization and channel model design
  • Key influencer and decision-maker engagement
  • Fractional business development leadership
  • Strategic buyer identification and outreach
04

Investor & Capital Strategy Support

Supporting Series A fundraising and investor relations through co-led pitch development, diligence preparation, and performance-based incentive structures.

  • Series A advisory and investor relations
  • Pitch development and diligence prep
  • Investor introduction and finder's fee structures
  • Stakeholder engagement and roadshow support
05

Market & Competitive Intelligence

Deep-dive competitive analysis and voice-of-customer insights to validate market position, identify white space, and sharpen differentiation.

  • Competitive landscape and market analysis
  • Voice-of-customer executive interviews
  • Market sizing and entry point analysis
  • Differentiator identification and validation
